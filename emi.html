EMI

If you are a merchant selling high-value goods or services, your customers might find it convenient to pay the total amount in installments. PayUmoney has partnered with various banks to provide this option to customers on our checkout pages so that your customers have one more reason to buy your product and thus enjoy very high success rates. Since EMI(Equated Monthly Installments) as an option is extended by Banks to their customers you as a merchant will get your settlements as per the agreed settlement cycle for the full amount without any delay. That is, other words, the entire credit risk is born by the banks. PayUmoney supports EMI Option for the following banks
